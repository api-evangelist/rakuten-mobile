This employee spotlight profiles Anindita Datta, AI Operations and Strategy Manager at Rakuten Symphony, tracing her career from mainframe engineering in India in 2007 through telecom solutions and relocation to Japan in 2019. At Rakuten Mobile she served as a Product Owner managing procurement systems before joining Rakuten Symphony and shifting toward program management, governance, and cross-team strategy. Datta advises newcomers to stay open and curious and not hesitate to ask questions.
